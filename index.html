ABOUT US

A Midland, Texas lifestyle website + online media company sharing things to do, local businesses, food, local deals, special events, and more for locals, transplants, and visitors alike with a collective online audience of 92K, reaching 150K locals per month.

WHY WORK WITH US

  • Hyperlocal, highly-engaged audience
  • High-quality, unique content with an authentic voice
  • Precise campaign metrics to provide reach, demographics, etc.
  • Selective of our sponsors in order to maintain the integrity of our brand and ensure effectiveness of sponsorship
